The Language Processing Lab is based in Department of Linguistics and Modern Languages and Brain and Mind Institute, The Chinese University of Hong Kong. We are interested in language comprehension, production and learning. Members of the lab have worked on the following topics among others.


  1. Syntactic representations in language processing
  2. Interlocutor modelling in language comprehension and production
  3. Language prediction during interpreting and translation
  4. Human-computer language communication
  5. Chinese handwriting literacy
